OUR TEAM

Canales Auty is a team of natural resources lawyers with top-tier reputation in advisory and contentious matters. Members of the firm have devoted their entire professional life to the study and practice of resources law.

Canales Auty is a boutique law firm dedicated exclusively to Latin America. Members of the firm advice private corporations, independent and major oil companies, oilfield service providers, lenders, sponsors and developers of greenfield and brownfield projects, government agencies and other law firms across Latin America.

Canales Auty combines academic credentials from leading universities with experience from private firms and international organisations. Everyone in the team has at least one post-graduate degree. Within the group there is English, French, Spanish and Portuguese language proficiency, with most members speaking at least two languages.
