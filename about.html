ABOUT THE FIRM

Canales Auty is a team of natural resources lawyers, with top-tier reputation in advisory and contentious matter.

Members of the firm have devoted their entire professional careers to the study and practice of natural resources law. Our lawyers work in diverse and multi-disciplinary teams to provide clients with the entire range of legal services related to natural resource development. We are the only boutique firm dedicated exclusively to natural resources law in Latin America.

Canales Auty core practice include: oil and gas, energy project development, liquefied natural gas, power generation and transmission, alternative and nuclear energy, regulatory, mining, water project development, environmental, waste and remediation.

Our oil and gas practice covers a gambit of downstream, midstream and upstream companies, including producers, processors, transporters, distributors and marketers. We are very active in advising E&P clients in Mexico, Ecuador, Colombia, Peru and Uruguay.

We hold market-leading regulatory, water and environmental experience to complement our oil and gas, energy and natural resources practice; including enforcement actions, environmental auditing, land development restrictions, regulatory representation, permitting and approvals.

Canales Auty has advised on numerous power generation and transmission projects including gas, geothermal, nuclear and renewable sources. We are well positioned to advise on complex regulatory matters including market design, operations and enforcement.

Our public international law practice is focused solely on oil and gas and natural resources matters, including contentious and non-contentious advice on investment treaties, price revisions under long-term contracts, maritime boundary delimitation, shared natural resources, joint development and international unitization agreements.

Our deeper understanding of our clients' industries has earned Canales Auty the privilege of working on some of the most complex legal matters across Latin America. We have proven experience advising governments, international organizations and private entities in countries such as Mexico, Bolivia, Chile, Colombia, Venezuela, Ecuador, Peru and Uruguay. Our unrelenting commitment to client service has contributed to the firm's exceptional reputation as a go-to natural resources law firm.
